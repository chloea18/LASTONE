What does this progam look like?

On a transformative journey through Europe during spring 2024, students have the opportunity to:

  1. Spend three weeks in each of the vibrant capitals: London, Paris, and Rome.

  2. Immerse themselves in a dynamic learning environment alongside cohorts of approximately twenty students.

  3. Rotate through classes crucial for majors and minors in the Marriott School of Business, including:
    • Accounting 200
    • Marketing 201
    • Global Supply Chain 201/211

  4. Gain firsthand exposure to the inner workings of multinational corporations such as:
    • Amazon
    • Disney
    • Microsoft
    • MiniCooper/BMW

  5. Immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of European culture by visiting iconic landmarks including:
    • Colosseum
    • Vatican
    • Eiffel Tower
    • Buckingham Palace
